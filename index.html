Nice to meet you!

Perhaps you have heard on the internet that I am an aspiring web developer and all-around OK guy. I have been living in the sunny Phoenix AZ area since 1997.

I am currently a Sr. Analyst with Verizon, responsible for maintenance and development of web applications supporting our Consumer Operations group. I work with Coldfusion, Teradata, SQL Server, VBA and more on a daily basis. Development includes web applications, email automation, SQL reporting and more. I am challenged every day and I love it!

Most recently, I have started study of web development. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node, MVC design, Databases, ORM, and more. There is so much to learn and I know I have only scratched the surface! My journey began with a webDev bootcamp from University of Arizona. You can see some of my work below.

In my analog life, I enjoy hiking with my wife and 11 year-old son, travel, disc golf, and taking care of my two fish tanks.
